What is More Recipe?
More-Recipes provides a platform for YOU to share the awesome and exciting recipe ideas you have invented or learnt.
So if you comes up with a recipe, you can post it on More-Recipes and
get feedback in form of reviews and votes from other users like you who have explored and used that recipe.
Also you can also keep a list of your favorite recipes on the application for future use.
